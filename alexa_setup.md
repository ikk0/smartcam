* Log into [Alexa Developer Console](https://developer.amazon.com/edw/home.html#/)
* Click Alexa Skills Kit
* Add a new skill
* Tab "Skill Information"
* Name: Smart Camera
* Invocation Name: smart camera
* Click "Next"
* Tab "Interaction Model":
